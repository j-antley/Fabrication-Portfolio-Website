ABOUT

June is a designer and fabricator based in Pittsburgh. They have 7 years of experience in fabrication, with a focus on welding, machining, and metal finishing techniques. Their skill set includes welding machining, carpentry, coding, auto mechanics, exhibit installation, and furniture design. Their work emphasizes the qualities innate to the materials they use, and they employ bold lines and solid structure. with a focus on material choice, high-end metal finishing techniques and architectural design.
