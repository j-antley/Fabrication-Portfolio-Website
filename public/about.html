JUNE A

About

June is a designer and fabricator based in Pittsburgh. They have 7 years of experience in fabrication, with a focus in metalwork. Their skill set includes welding, machining, carpentry, coding, CNC routing, auto mechanics, exhibit installation, and furniture design.Their work emphasizes bold lines, exposed hardware, durability, and defensive structure, drawing inspiration from Brutalist and Italo-futurist traditions, medieval reliquaries, and their own affinity for rust-belt industrialism. They utilize high-end metal finishing techniques and architectural design to showcase the qualities of their raw materials.
